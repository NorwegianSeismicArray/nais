nais.Mixture module¶

@author: Olivier Algoet @summary:

Causality model defining two gaussian mixture models in tensorflow model gives probability of state/observations/other given failure/normal operation –> p(x|f) and p(x|n_o)

@legend:

k cluster amount N batch size D dimensions of state x | observation z
